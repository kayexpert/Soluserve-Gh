who we are

SOLUSERV (GH) LIMITED is a 100% locally owned Ghanaian Company. We are registered with the Ghana Petroleum Commission to provide services to the Oil and Gas sector in Ghana. The company has built capacity through the development of a robust Well Operations Management System, Safety Management System and Quality Control and Assurance System. The company’s management systems and procedures have been audited and an Integrated Management System Certification, (ISO 9001: 2015 for Quality Management System; ISO 14001:2015 for Environmental Management System; and ISO 45001:2018 for Occupational Health & Safety System) has been issued to guide its operations.

Our mission

Our mission is to become an industry leader in the provision of varied well operations & intervention services and well operations & engineering management by providing a cost effective, quality engineered solution to our clients.

Our Vision

  1. As we aspire to be one of leading locally owned upstream oil and gas service provider in Ghana and the subregion, our goal is to endeavor to produce well trained, motivated and competent staff to deliver quality product and service.
  2. Pursue and assure our objective of no harm to people and the environment.
